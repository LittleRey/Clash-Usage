## 网易云教程

### Windows网易云
1.打开Clash,找到网易云代理组，选择带有绿色数字的节点

<img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/LittleRey/NET-Usage/Netease/1.png" width="75%">
2.打开网易云点击设置，点击工具，勾选IE代理，点击确定重启网易云

<img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/LittleRey/NET-Usage/Netease/2.png" width="75%">
3.打开网易云随便搜个周杰伦的歌听

<img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/LittleRey/NET-Usage/Netease/3.png" width="75%">

### Android网易云
1.打开Clash,找到网易云代理组，选择带有延迟的节点，然后打开网易云直接听

<img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/LittleRey/NET-Usage/Netease/4.png" width="35%">
